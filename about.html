About us

Space is one of the greatest discoveries in human history. In the past, people only admired the lights they saw in the sky, but as technology has developed, interest and research in Space has gradually increased. We are a community that shares our curiosity and fascination with Space. Our aim is to bring together all those who follow Space exploration and research, who want to learn and share knowledge in this field. In this about us article about Space, we would like to explain what we do and what we can offer about Space. Our site offers articles and videos covering topics such as Space exploration, observations, science and technology news. We also have a large library of information about planets, stars, galaxies and other celestial bodies. In addition, we aim to increase interest in Space with Space observation guides, photo galleries and other interactive content. With the contribution of our site visitors, we are building a community to learn and explore more about Space. Space is an endless journey of discovery. Our website is a reflection of our curiosity about Space and we would be happy to see everyone who wants to share this curiosity among us. We have come together as a community sharing our curiosity about Space. Our aim is to learn and share information about Space exploration and research and to increase interest in Space. Our website contains articles, videos, photo galleries and other interactive content about Space discoveries, observations, science and technology news, information about planets, stars, galaxies and other celestial bodies. Space is an endless journey of discovery and we invite you to this journey.
